# DriveLoop - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 28, 2026

## Introduction

DriveLoop ("we", "our", or "the app") is a dashcam application developed for Android devices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

**Your privacy is important to us. DriveLoop is designed to store all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share your personal data with any third parties or external servers.**

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Camera and Video Data
- **What:** Video recordings from your device's camera
- **Why:** To provide dashcam functionality for recording driving footage
- **Storage:** Stored locally on your device in the app's dedicated folder
- **Retention:** Managed by you; old recordings are automatically deleted based on your storage quota settings

### 2. Audio Data (Optional)
- **What:** Audio recordings captured alongside video
- **Why:** To record in-cabin audio during driving
- **Storage:** Stored locally as part of video files
- **Control:** You can enable or disable audio recording in the app settings

### 3. Location Data (Optional)
- **What:** GPS coordinates, speed, altitude, and bearing
- **Why:** To overlay telemetry information on video recordings and display current speed
- **Storage:** Stored locally in sidecar files (.json and .srt) alongside video recordings
- **Control:** You can enable or disable GPS logging in the app settings

### 4. Accelerometer Data
- **What:** Motion sensor data from device accelerometer
- **Why:** To detect sudden impacts or collisions for emergency recording
- **Storage:** Not stored; processed in real-time only
- **Control:** You can enable or disable emergency recording in the app settings

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only
All data collected by DriveLoop is stored **exclusively on your device**. We do not:
- Upload any data to cloud servers
- Share data with third parties
- Collect analytics or usage statistics
- Use advertising SDKs

### Storage Location
- Video files are stored in: `Android/data/com.yekkaplan.driveloop/files/Movies/DriveLoop/`
- Emergency recordings are stored in a separate `Emergency/` subfolder

### Data Retention
- You control data retention through the Storage Quota setting (2-50 GB)
- When the quota is reached, the oldest recordings are automatically deleted
- Emergency recordings are preserved and not automatically deleted

---

## Permissions Required

DriveLoop requires the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| **Camera** | To record video footage |
| **Microphone** | To record audio (optional, can be disabled) |
| **Location** | To log GPS data and display speed (optional, can be disabled) |
| **Storage** | To save video recordings to your device |
| **Foreground Service** | To continue recording when the app is in the background |
| **Wake Lock** | To keep the device awake during recording |
| **Notifications** | To show recording status in the notification bar |

---

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

**We do not share your data with any third parties.**

DriveLoop:
- Does not contain advertisements
- Does not use analytics services
- Does not communicate with external servers
- Does not integrate with social media platforms

The only way your data leaves your device is if you manually share a video file using the system share feature.

## Children's Privacy

DriveLoop is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your Rights

As all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:
- **Access:** All recordings are accessible through the app's Files section
- **Deletion:** You can delete individual recordings or all data at any time
- **Export:** You can share/export any recording using the system share feature
- **Disable:** You can disable any optional data collection (audio, GPS) in settings
